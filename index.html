About Me

I am a graduate of Mississippi State University who now resides in the Dallas/Fort Worth area. I moved to Dallas/Fort Worth from Memphis three years ago. My favorie part about living in the Dallas/Fort Worth area is the fact that a few of my best friends live here and have helped me to make new friends. I am currently studying web development at SMU.

My parents and younger brother still live in Memphis. My parents were in the pharmaceutical industry for many years and went to pharmacy school at Purdue University. My brother is a Mechanical Engineer and is a graduate of Texas A&M University. My family means everything to me and I visit them whenever I can. Without them, I would not be the person that I am today.

I like playing sports in order to stay active. I also run and ride my bicycle frequently for my other main forms of physical activity. I also like attending sporting events, especially Dallas Stars games. I also enjoy travelling because it allows me to see more of the country and the world. I am especially happy when I get to travel with family or friends. It is my goal to see as much of the world as I can in my lifetime.
